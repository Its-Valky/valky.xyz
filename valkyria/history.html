History

Castanic

Capital: Castanica - Planet: Arborea

The whole race Castanic suffered great oppression and prejudice for allying themselves with the Demon God Lok. As a result, they have lost their trust of outsiders and decided to do things by themselves instead. Most Castanics follow no gods anymore, although some have decided to follow Valky, Goddess of Freedom. The humans helped Valky to settle the Castanic free from all suffering, but only after they were free from the demon's grasp.

The Castanic are originally a clan who fled the bloodthirsty Devan Empire, and their Goddess of Darkness, Zuras. Castanics wandered the continent of Arun on the planet Arborea and founded the capital city Castanica until Lok took them under his patronage, as Castanics were skilled artisans and to annoy his sister Zuras. Lok created with the best Castanic artisans an artifact known as the Crown of Remedy. The crown was meant cure Zuras and channel the collected power of the gods from her and store it somewhere – but it ended up killing her. This caused not only the arcane scars on the Castanics bodies which persist to this day as sort of a Mark of Cain. This also caused the Castanics to abandon their home in Castanica and make them flee to diffrent planets.

Human

Capital: Valkyria - Planet: Earth

Cursed by their god to wander without a homeland, humans turned their adversity to advantage by using their long exile as a learning experience. They studied the culture and crafts of other races, and forged their own destiny without guidance from their neglectful deity. Now, as creators of Project Valkyria, they are admired and respected by all the nations of the world for their role in bringing together the coalition that will defeat the Demon God Lok and return the planet Arborea back to peace at last.

Gavin, God of Wisdom, created the human race but turned his back on them when they petitioned him for a homeland. When the first humans conspired to steal Gavin's priceless treasures, he cursed them to wander forever. As the godly war drew to a close, the curse on the humans lifted. Valky, Goddess of Freedom, took pity on the humans and granted them the homeland they’d long been denied. Their capital today is the beautiful city Valkyria she created with them.

Elves

Capital: Uamia - Planet: Yubarath

Noble yet cunning, Elves are known for their arrogance and great knowledge. Elves were hostile and expanding though conquest. They were the pinnacle of civilization before the godly war. However, after the war's source of power, the Flower of Life, got destroyed, it causes massive destruction and many deaths. The Elves split to two factions, the Wind Elves and the Mighty Elves.

The Elves once lived lives dedicated to venerating nature and the gods. The children of Alina, Goddess of Nature, maintained their forest homes in peace, but that changed when the conflicts of immortals destroyed their civilization and split a proud and powerful race into two factions. Now, only the Wind Elves remain. After hundred years of conquest, a force of allied races dealt the Mighty Elves armies a devastating loss. After this rebuke, the Elves withdrew from the world for a century of seclusion that only ended when the gates of Uamia opened to refugees fleeing the invasion of the Demon God Lok. Valky, Goddess of Freedom, took notice when from those gates marched an army that fought for the lives of all mortal races. Though the blood of Elven soldiers bought their seat at the table of Project Valkyria, many other races do not trust the Elves new way, in believing the capital's walls conceal many secrets and that the Elves never gave up their conquering ways.
