25. Feb 2020


Bug:
Instead of only horizontal rotation, player model can be angled verticaly. At least it was never that easy to win in Limbo!
20-02-26 / Solution:
Created new independent onMouseMove function and disabled pitchObject.rotation() imported by Three.JS

Bug:
New feature! You can bug through collision boxes and fall through the map - just in case someone wants to know whats below the ground.
20-02-27 / Solution:
Changed Phys-X and Light / Shadow settings. More infos here:
Phys-X Changes:
- Increased player collision radius from 10 to 15
- Increased player collision mass from 1 to 5
- Increased ground collision box height from 0 to 0.1
- Increased ground collision mass from 5 to 25
- Decreased world gravity from 49 to 39
Light and Shadow Changes:
- Decreased shadow intensity from 8192px/m2 to 2048px/m2
- Removed shadow smoothing functionality
DEVELOPER NOTE:
Bug not fully solved yet, player can bug through collision boxes like houses when frames are dropping, but they cant fall through the map anymore because of the higher mass and lower gravity. The new Shadow and Light settings increase performance by around ~30%
